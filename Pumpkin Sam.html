A Cute Little Story For Kids

Pumpkin Sam

Once there lived a Farmer. He grew three pumpkin in his field.
A Monkey helped the Farmer in the field. When the three pumpkin were ripe

A pig came and began to eat them. The Farmer and the Monkey thought for a

Solution. They decided to make a Scarecrow to keep the pig away. The

Monkey stuffed hay into a apnt and a shirt. The head of the Scarecrow was made

of a sack filled with hay. They put a hat on its head. Then the Monkey drew a face

on the head. Now, the Scarecrow was ready. They named PUMPKIN SAM.
