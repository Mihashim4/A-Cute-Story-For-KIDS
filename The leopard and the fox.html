The Leopard And The Fox

One day the leopard and the fox were chatting by the riverside.
The leopard looked at his reflection in the river and said,

"My! What a charming,silky coat I have on me! And look at he spots on them

As for you, you look dull and grey!"

The fox was quite nice to look at. He had a bushy, long tail and
long pointy ears.But he could never look as handsome as the leopard.

"You may have a nice coat and look smart.I know I do not look as handsome
as you, but I am smart in my head. Real smartness lies in not arguing with

foolish creatures. I do not wish to waste my time quarrelling about looks,"

said the fox as he walked away.

Moral
IT IS MORE IMPORTANT TO BE SMART THAN BE SMART IN APPEARENCE.
