A Cute Little Moral Story For Kids

The Goat and the Fox

There lived two goats named Jiku and Biku. They were great friends.
Even the foxes were scared of these robust goats.

"I can never kill them in a direct attack. Let me find some trick to make them hate each other,"
the fox thought

On one of those days, the fox saw Biku grazing alone.
He quickly approached the goat.

"Your friend Jiku told me that you are very quarrelsome and you ate a lot.
He also said that he doesn't like you at all," the fox said.

'Did Jiku say so?" Biku asked. "Yes. He also said that if he ever sees you again, he will kill you,"
the fox added

Precisely at that time, Jiku goat came there."Friend, I was worried when I didn't see you,"
Jiku said

Biku realized that the fox was trying to break their friendship.
He hit the fox with his horns and the scared fox ran away.

Moral
GOOD FRIENDS WILL NEVER BECOME ENEMIES.
