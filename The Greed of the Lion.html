A Cute Little Moral Story For Kids

The Greed Of The Lion

Once, there was a lion.
One day, while looking for food, he saw a rabbit.

"Wow, this made my day", the lion thought.
He chased the rabbit and in one leap captured it.

It was then he noticed a healthy deer.
"A deer is better than a rabbit!" he thought.

The lion discarded the rabbit and went after the deer.
But the deer could run very fast.

The lion could not catch the deer.
Thus he lost the rabbit he had and failed to get the deer.

The disappointed lion sadly went back to the forest
