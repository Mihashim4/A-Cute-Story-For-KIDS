A Cute Little Moral Story For Kids

The Hungry Mouse

Once upon a time, there lived a mouse named Strings. Strings had a very tough time. She had no food for many days and made strenuous efforts to get food. All her efforts went in vain. She looked everywhere to find food. As days passed by, she became very thin.

One day, the mouse found a basket filled with corn. She also noticed a tiny hole in the basket, which was just enough for her to enter. She easily moved inside the basket. Since she had no food for many days, she ate a large amount of corn. Without realizing it, she continued eating more and more. Soon, she became very fat!

After eating, the fat mouse tried to come out through the tiny hole. Unfortunately, the hole was too small for her now. The mouse started screaming, "Oh God! How can I come out of this basket?"

A rat heard the mouse screaming and asked what happened. The mouse told the whole story and asked for a solution. The rat replied, "If you want to come out of the basket, you must wait until you become thin again."

The mouse realized her mistake and started starving to escape from the basket.

Moral
Anything too much is good for nothing.
