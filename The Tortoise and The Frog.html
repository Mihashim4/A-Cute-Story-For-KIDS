A Cute Little Moral Story For Kids

The Tortoise and The Frog

A tortoise and a frog live in a pond
They always quarrelled.

Brother tortoise, you are at all good-looking.
You have a body like a rock and an ungly neck and face

The frog said

The tortoise smiled when he heard this.

You are also ugly with a pair of bulbous eyes.
Your body is covered with marks and you have such ugly legs,

The tortoise retorted.

Thus a fight broke out between them.
A fish was watching all this.

So the tortoise and the frog went to the fish.

Brother fish, can you please say who among us is handsome?
they asked.

Neither of you is good-looking. Don't you both speak more about
the faults of others than about their qualities? Good words and deeds

of a person are his real beauty, not a beautiful physical appearance,

the fish said

They both felt that the fish was right. From then on, they never
quarrelled again and became very good friends.

Moral
BEAUTY COMES FROM INSIDE NOT FROM OUTSIDE
